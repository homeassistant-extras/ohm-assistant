Shocking Energy Card - Mock Data Preview

About This Card

This card fetches and displays real energy consumption history from Home Assistant with two datasets:

  • 1-Minute Data (Blue): Shows power consumption in Watts from sensor.living_room_15_1min over the last 24 hours.
  • Daily Data (Green): Shows total energy consumption in kWh from sensor.living_room_15_1d over the last 30 days.

Note: This demo page won't work standalone - it needs to be loaded in Home Assistant to access the history API.

To use: Copy dist/shocking.js to your Home Assistant www folder and add the card to your dashboard.
